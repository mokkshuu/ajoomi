Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ajoomi.

How fast will a professional arrive?
Our Fast-Track booking connects you with a professional in as little as 30 minutes. For non-urgent tasks you can also schedule a convenient time slot.
Are all professionals background verified?
Yes. Every professional on Ajoomi goes through a thorough background check, identity verification, and skill assessment before being onboarded.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on service. All online payments are processed through secure gateways.
Is there a warranty on services?
Yes! All labor comes with a 7-day rework warranty. If you are not satisfied with the work, we will send the professional back at no extra charge.
Which cities does Ajoomi currently serve?
We currently operate in Bilaspur, Raipur, Korba, Durg, Bhilai, Rajnandgaon and are expanding rapidly across Chhattisgarh.
How do I become a service partner?
Click "Become a Partner" on the homepage, fill in your details, and our team will contact you within 24 hours to get you started.
